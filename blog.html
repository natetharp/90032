LA-32 NC Special Meeting of the Board

July 22, 2013

On July 25 at 6:00 PM the LA-32 Neighborhood Council will be holding a special meeting at the Barrio Action Center at 4927 Huntington Drive to review the proposed Eastern & Lombardy project.

There has been some speculation that some members of the Neighborhood Council will not show up to the meeting in an attempt to prevent the council from voting against the project, thereby overturning their previous votes in favor of the project.

In light of this LA-32 Neighborhood Council President, Connie Castro, sent the following email last Friday to remind the other council members of the responsibilities associated with their elected office (emphasis hers):

From: Connie Castro
To: LA-32 NC Board Members
Sent: Friday, July 19, 2013 9:31 PM
Subject: LA-32 NC Special Meeting of the Board June 25 at 6PM Barrio Action

Dear LA-32 NC Board-members,

I hope this email finds you well. As President and presiding officer of the LA-32 Neighborhood Council and in consultation with the City Attorney and the Department of Neighborhood Empowerment, I am calling for a Special Meeting of the new LA-32 NC Board in place to discuss the Development on 2520-2668 North Eastern Avenue (Case No. CPC-2011-3128-GPA-ZC-HD) the construction of a Charter Elementary Students (530 students), a 20 Unit multi-family residential structure and a Commercial Space (caf√©), said property is currently zoned Residential.

The LA-32 Neighborhood Council has received numerous emails and comments from both the Developer and the Residents being impacted by this project. As you are all aware it is our obligation to inform and educate, to the greatest extent possible, all Stakeholders of forthcoming projects and diligently outreach to the diverse members of the community. Lets not forget that the purpose of LA-32 NC is to participate as a body on issues concerning our neighborhood and represent the interest of all LA-32 NC members ("Stakeholders") regarding issues affecting the LA-32 NC area. As such our responsibility as board members is to promote and enhance civic participation and community affairs ensuring the opportunity for diverse interests to participate in those affairs of the community.

Renewed community interest and increased stakeholder participation in regards to this project deserve our immediate attention to this matter.

Please plan on attending this very important meeting. The Board will entertain a motion from Board members on whether to support, oppose, or take no position on the proposed project. The meeting will take place this upcoming Thursday July 25, 2013 at Barrio Action. Please arrive on time as the meeting will begin promptly at 6pm.

Connie Castro
President, LA-32 Neighborhood Council
